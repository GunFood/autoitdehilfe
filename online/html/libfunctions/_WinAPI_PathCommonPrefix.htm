_WinAPI_PathCommonPrefix

Compares two paths to determine if they share a common prefix

#include <WinAPIShPath.au3>
_WinAPI_PathCommonPrefix ( $sPath1, $sPath2 )

Parameter

$sPath1 The first path name.
$sPath2 The second path name.

Rückgabewert

Returns the common prefix, @extended flag will contain the count of common prefix characters in the path.

Bemerkungen

None.

Siehe auch

Suche nach PathCommonPrefix in der MSDN Bibliothek.

Beispiel

#include <WinAPIShPath.au3>

Local $aPath1 = 'C:\Documents\Test.txt'
Local $aPath2 = 'C:\Documents\Archive\Sample.txt'

ConsoleWrite('Pfad1 : ' & $aPath1 & @CRLF)
ConsoleWrite('Pfad2 : ' & $aPath2 & @CRLF)
ConsoleWrite('Präfix: ' & _WinAPI_PathCommonPrefix($aPath1, $aPath2) & @CRLF)
